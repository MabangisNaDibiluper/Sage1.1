When anxiety arrives, do these in order.
01
First
Pause & Acknowledge
Name what you're feeling. Say it out loud or in your head: "I am feeling anxious right now, and that is okay." Naming it reduces its power.
⏱ 30 seconds
02
Second
Breathe Slowly
Inhale for 4 counts, hold for 4, exhale for 6. Repeat 3–5 times. This activates your parasympathetic nervous system and calms the stress response.
⏱ 2 minutes
03
Third
Ground Yourself
Name 5 things you can see, 4 you can touch, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste. This pulls your mind back into the present moment.
⏱ 3 minutes
04
Fourth
Move Your Body
Even 5 minutes of walking, stretching, or gentle movement releases cortisol and shifts your energy. You don't need a workout — just move.
⏱ 5 minutes
05
Fifth
Hydrate & Rest
Drink a full glass of water slowly. Anxiety often dehydrates us. Find a quiet place to sit, remove stimulants, and allow your nervous system to settle.
⏱ 5 minutes
06
Sixth
Express It
Write it down, talk to someone, or add a note to your Sage wall. Externalizing the feeling removes it from the loop in your mind.

Sage was born from a quiet belief — that everyone deserves a space to say what they've been holding inside. We built this with care, with empathy, and with you in mind.

Our Mission

"To create a gentle, safe space where people can speak their unsaid feelings, find connection, and begin to heal — without fear of judgment."
